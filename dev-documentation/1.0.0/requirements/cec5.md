# Circular Enabler Category 4 – The capacity to manage the system

This caregory includes 2 specific stories and 17 CQs.

## Requirements

| ID | Description  | CQs | Reasoning Requirements |
|---|---|---|---|
| CE12 | The capacity to coordinate processes and actors for the benefit of the system. Implementation actions: The ability to manage risk in case of exchange failure (in material flows). The ability to manage energy exchanges decentralised. The ability to make decisions (on energy flows) automatically. The ability to establish shared vision (on value) and align objectives. The ability to ensure that responsibilities and obligations are met (to create value). | [CQ-CE-12-1](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-12-1), [CQ-CE-12-2](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-12-2), [CQ-CE-12-3](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-12-3), [CQ-CE-12-4](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-12-4), [CQ-CE-12-5](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-12-5), [CQ-CE-12-6](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-12-6), [CQ-CE-12-7](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-12-7), [CQ-CE-12-8](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-12-8), [CQ-CE-12-9](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-12-9), [CQ-CE-12-10](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-12-10), [CQ-CE-12-11](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-12-11) | A CVN may not be centrally coordinated or governed by a formal agreement, hence, most information on responsibilities, objectives etc may only be implicitly derivable from other data. Contingency plans/options and best options may be derivable from descriptions of the needs and characteristics of processes. |
| CE13 | The capacity to interact and share information with actors in an effective and trustful way. Implementation actions: The ability to share information transparently and traceably. The ability to standardise material information. The ability to verify value creation. | [CQ-CE-13-1](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-13-1), [CQ-CE-13-2](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-13-2), [CQ-CE-13-3](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-13-3), [CQ-CE-13-4](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-13-4), [CQ-CE-13-5](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-13-5), [CQ-CE-13-6](../competency-questions/cec5.md#cq-ce-13-6) | The responsibilities, history, access rights, verification status etc can be inherited from a larger collection of information, e.g. a document or data sheet, so the statements contained in it. Unless something is stated to be verified, it is treated as not verified. |
